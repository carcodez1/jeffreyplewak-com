Root `AGENTS.md` applies first; this file adds stricter recruiter-route rules only.

Role of `/r`:
- `/r` is the recruiter decision route
- it is not the canonical resume
- it is not a redirect
- it should route a recruiter quickly to the correct next action

Priority order on `/r`:
1. `/resume`
2. recruiter-pack artifacts
3. `/projects/kprovengine` proof

Content rules:
- keep the hero human, calm, and recruiter-facing
- prioritize identity, fit, proof, then downloads
- avoid internal architecture language in visible copy
- keep content HTML-first and easy to scan
- KProvEngine is the only proof card in Phase 1
- do not imply this repo is the canonical KProvEngine source repository

Do not add in Phase 1 unless explicitly approved:
- analytics
- matcher/report widget
- recruiter-specific JSON-LD unless derived directly from visible content with zero claim drift
